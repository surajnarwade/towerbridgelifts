About this project

Where does the data come from?

The data is automatically scraped from the official Tower Bridge website using a custom script.

To respect the official server's resources, our automated process runs only once every 6 hours to ensure the information is up-to-date while minimizing load on the origin.

Why I made it?

Tower Bridge is one of London's most iconic landmarks. Watching the bridge lift is a unique experience, but the official timetable can sometimes be hidden away or hard to read on the go.

I built this dashboard to provide a fast, modern, and mobile-friendly way to check lift times, with the added convenience of adding them to your personal calendar instantly.
